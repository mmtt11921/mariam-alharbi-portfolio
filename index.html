02 · About Mariam

“Technology becomes meaningful when it is built around people, progress, and possibility.”

Mariam Alharbi is a technology builder shaping a multidisciplinary path across cybersecurity, healthcare AI, and information systems. Her work is grounded in a practical belief: meaningful digital solutions begin with a deep understanding of people and the systems around them.

Alongside product concepts and future ventures, she creates learning communities that make knowledge easier to explore, apply, and share. Her evolving portfolio reflects a founder mindset: curious, intentional, and committed to building ideas that can grow into lasting value.
